Our Story

Founded in 2020, Urban Edge Barbershop was built on a simple idea — to combine the timeless art of barbering with a touch of modern sophistication. We believe that every man deserves to look sharp, feel confident, and express his style.

Our barbers are professionals who take pride in their craft. From traditional fades to the latest haircut trends, we deliver precision, care, and comfort in every session. At Urban Edge, you’re not just getting a haircut — you’re experiencing the lifestyle.
